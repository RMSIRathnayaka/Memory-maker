Memory Maker, a professional association event company, has experience working with associations to address issues like low member involvement, shifting regulatory environments, and student disinterest. The Memory Maker team provides a diverse range of services to manage various association events, including:

  • Annual general meetings
  • Annual conference, conventions or symposiums
  • Professional development events for members
  • Educational seminars
  • Informational sessions
  • Student engagement events
  • Monthly member chapter dinner or lunch meetings
  • Industry tradeshows
