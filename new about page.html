NSBM Green University

National School of Business Management (NSBM) Green University is the first ever green university in South Asia and sets an example for the whole South Asia by paving the way for environmental sustainability. This university found in 2011. The Vice-Chancellor: Prof. E A Weerasinghe and Deputy Vice-Chancellor: Prof.Chaminda Rathnayake. NSBM is a Government-owned institute. This state-of-the-art university offers nationally and internationally recognized, UGC-approved degree programmes and foreign degree programmes in four faculties: Business, Computing, Engineering, Science, and Postgraduate Studies.
